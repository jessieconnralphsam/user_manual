Quick Guide: Creating Your Account in 3 Simple Steps

Effortlessly set up your account with our step-by-step guide in just three easy steps. Follow along to access seamless system support and ticket management.

Accessing the Registration Page
  • Go to the official ticketing website or the instance provided by your organization.
  • Look for the "Create an Account" option, located on the homepage.
Filling out the Registration Form
  • Fill in the required information, including your email address, preferred username, and a secure password.
  • Click on the "Register" button to proceed.
Account Confirmation and Verification
  • Check your email inbox for a confirmation message from helpdesk.
  • Access your email and select the provided verification link to confirm the activation of your account. For new users, the Helpdesk may furnish a temporary password, necessitating a subsequent password change for security reasons.
  • Log in using the credentials you provided during registration.
